PRODUCT COMPARISON

  1. Home
  2. Product Comparison
  • Braking Performance [BP]

    Street Series uses a specific formulated material and a drilled & slotted pattern which will increase the friction coefficient and 20% better cooling compared to an OEM brake disc. Street Series also has a high initial bite characteristics that’s fitting for a day-to-day use.

    Competition Series from BRIX Performance is designed with an unique slotted pattern that functions as a performance optimalization so the braking sensation will feel more linear and predictable with higher friction coefficient. It’s best used for competition or drivers who wants higher control.

  • Resistance to Crack [RC]

    Street series has a chamfer on their holes and uses a material a special formula and a SOLIDCAST heat-treatment process, that will minimize the possibility of micro-cracking and heat-cracking when used in a high temperature. It’s suitable for high performance cars.

    With the use of FC250 in BRIX Competition Series and a special slotted pattern, it will maximalise braking during high temperatures and it will significantly minimalize the probability of heat-checking or micro-cracking in the disc brake when used for tracks and competitions.

  • Initial Bite [IB]

    The initial bite in the Street Series will feel better due to the use of a special drilled & slotted pattern. This makes the braking feels more gripping for street uses.

    Competition Series and its unique slotted pattern and FC250 High Carbon material will maximalise the pedal feel to be more linear and predictable, with a higher initial bite than a standard disc brake but lower compared to the street series.

  • Vibration Damping (Noise) [VD]

    Street series with its drilled & slotted pattern and a special material has the ability of higher vibration damping, making it more comfortable to use in a daily car. This ability is higher compared to a standard disc brake.

    With a new pattern and material in Competition Series, the vibration damping is better especially in a hard braking. The specific pattern used also reduce the sound noise from the disc brake in higher speed.

  • Pad Life [PL]

    Street Series, with a drilled & slotted pattern, makes the braking performance and cooling better than the standard disc brake, with slightly higher use of brake pad compared to a standard disc.

    In Competition Series, it will have a slight reduction for the Pad Life due to the harsh material and more aggressive pattern that’s focused on competition use. To maximize the use of this brake disc on competitions, then it’ll affect the brake pad’s usage time.

  • Disc Life [DL]

    Street Series uses a specific material that’s formulated for daily cars that wanted high performance, with a harsher material and higher resilience compared to OEM disc brakes. Street Series is best partnered with an organic brake pad, with a more aggressive ceramic and semi-metallic upgrade.

    Competition Series uses a FC250 High Carbon material that’s harder and has a high resilience for repeated high temperatures and the use of semi-metallic or metallic brake pads for competitions that’s aggressive. This material can endure twice or more compared to OEM Disc brakes with the use of racing brake pads.

  • Corrosion Resistance [CR]

    Competition Series and Street Series has a special layer that minimalize corrosion due to oxidation and other chemical reactions. Street Series has an anti-corrosion layer with a silver colour that looks clean, and the ones that were stricken by disc pads will disappear on its own.

    Competition Series uses a thinner anti-corrosion layer on the friction side which will result in a faster bedding in process during the competition. This special layer will minimalize surface rust on the disc brake.

  • Dimensional Stability [DS]

    Dimensional stability in the Street Series is way higher compared to a standard brake disc because the use material used is formulated specially for higher heat and high performance on the street. It minimalize the risk of disc warping and run-out on the disc brake surface that resulted in vibration.

    Competition Series with its FC250 material has a slightly better ability compared to Street Series to maintain its dimension when used in a higher temperature. It minimalize the risk of disc warping and run-out on the disc brake surface that resulted in vibration.

  • Pedal Feel [PF]

    The Face Pattern in Street Series uses a drilled & slotted pattern that’s suitable for daily cars that wants a better braking performance. This pattern maximalise the pedal feel with a better initial bite.

    Competition Series uses a slotted pattern that’s formulized specially with a dashed pattern, which makes the pedal feel more linear and predictable for better control and repetitive usage.
