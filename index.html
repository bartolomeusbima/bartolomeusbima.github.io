SOLIDCAST®


SOLIDCAST® : Heat-treatment can make or break a brake disc. That is why BRIX Performance developed our own heat-treatment technique for brake rotors, which relieves internal stresses introduced into the rotor during after casting and after CNC machining. This process makes our brake rotors more resistant to warp and can sustain repeated high temperature use or lots of thermal cycling, with reduced disc wear, even when paired with an aggressive brake pad, meaning reliable brakes whenever you need it thanks to the SOLIDCAST® technique.

BRIX has always been committed to research innovative solutions for motorsports, certified under TUV/ISO/TS16949, continue to provide the best braking system for Indonesian’s market to date,
