SOLIDCAST®


SOLIDCAST® : Heat-treatment can make or break a brake disc. That’s why BRIX Performance develop our own heat-treatment technique for brake rotors, which relieves internal stresses induced into the rotor during after casting and after CNC machining. This process makes our brake rotors more resistant to warp and can sustain repeated high temperature use or lots of thermal cycling with reduced disc wear, even when paired with an aggressive brake pad. This means reliable brakes whenever you need it thanks to the SOLIDCAST® technique.

BRIX Performance has always been committed to research and develop innovative solutions for motorsports, certified under TUV/ISO/TS16949, we provide the best braking system for the Indonesian market to date.
